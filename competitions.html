Who We Are
About Science Spark

Science Spark is a youth organization dedicated to spreading science, creativity, and innovation among students. We inspire young minds through science projects, quizzes, olympiads, workshops, and educational events.

We create opportunities for students to explore knowledge, develop skills, and build confidence in science and leadership — shaping a generation of curious, talented, and socially responsible future leaders.
