Who We Are
Our Story

Science Spark is a youth organization dedicated to spreading science, creativity, and innovation among students. The organization inspires young minds through science projects, quizzes, olympiads, workshops, and educational events.

It creates opportunities for students to explore knowledge, develop skills, and build confidence in science and leadership. Science Spark aims to build a generation of curious, talented, and socially responsible future leaders.

We operate in three key ways: we organize our own competitions, we become members of other clubs and help them grow, and we assist organizations in gathering participants for their events.
